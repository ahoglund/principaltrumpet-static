The Bible Says...

Who is God?

God is the Creator of the universe and everything that is in it. The Bible says in Genesis 1:1 " In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth." See also Genesis 1:27, Genesis 2:7. In John 1:1-4 we read " In the beginning was the Word and the Word was with God and the Word was God. He was with God in the beginning. Through Him all things were made; without Him nothing was made that has been made. In Him was life, and that life was the light of men."
