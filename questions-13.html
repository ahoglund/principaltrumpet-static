The Bible Says...

How do I talk to God?

We talk to God through prayer. Prayer is a conversation with God. Jesus spoke to us about prayer and gave us an example of how to pray in Matthew 6:5-13 when He said, "Our Father in heaven, hallowed be Your name,Your kingdom come, Your will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us today our daily bread. Forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from the evil one." See also Jer. 33:3, John16:24, Matt.7:7
