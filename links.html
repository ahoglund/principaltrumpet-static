Featured Recording


Contest Solos
This CD features music for the young trumpet player. Are you looking for solos to be used in competitions, or on a recital? Here are some suggestions. Philip Smith and Joseph Turrin perform the following featured pieces: Turrin, Caprice; Ropartz, Andante et Allegro; Barat, Andante et Scherzo; Delmas, Choral et Variations; Corelli/Fitzgerald, Sonata VIII; Bozza, Badinage; Latham, Suite; Clarke, Maid of the Mist; Alexius, Sonatina; Goedicke, Concert Etude; Young, Contempora Suite; Turrin, Three Episodes; Handel/Fitzgerald, Aria con variazioni; and Broughton, Oliver's Birthday.
