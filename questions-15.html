New York Legends
Philip Smith and Joseph Turrin joined by a host of great players to present an exciting and varied program of standard and new works. Included pieces: Broughton, Concertpiece for Eight Trumpets; Turrin, Intrada; Enescu, Legende; Tomasi, Tryptyque; MacDowell, To A Wild Rose; Curnow, Amazing Grace; Gershwin, Someone to Watch Over Me; Turrin, Festival Fanfare; Bernstein, Rondo for Lifey and Fanfare for Bima; Lane, Song for Cornet; Ewazen, Trio for Trumpet, Violin and Piano; Turrin, Two Portraits; Thompson, There's a Great Day Coming.

The Bible Says...

How do I know that I have a relationship with God and eternal life?

The Bible tells us that when we bellieve in God the Father and Jesus Christ, the Son, we have a relationship and will have eternal life. In I John 5:11 we read, " And this is the testimony: God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son (Jesus Christ), He who has the Son has life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have life." See also John 5:25, Romans 10:9
