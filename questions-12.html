The Bible Says...

How do I ask Jesus into my life?

The Bible tells us that all we have to do is simply ask Jesus to come into our hearts, confess our sins to Him and believe in Him. The Bible says in Romans 10:9-11, "That if you confess with your mouth, "Jesus is Lord," and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved. For it is with your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you confess and are saved. As the Scripture says, "Anyone who trusts in Him will never be put to shame." Acts 16:31 says, "Believe in the Lord Jesus, and you will be saved."
