The Bible Says...

If God loves me, why do I feel separated from Him?

Our unconfessed sin and unbelief in Jesus Christ keeps us separated from God. The Bible says in Isaiah 59:1-2 \"Surely the arm of the LORD is not too short to save, nor His ear too dull to hear. But your iniquities have separated you from your God; your sins have hidden His face from you, so that He will not hear.\" See also, Romans 3:23 and John 3:18-21
