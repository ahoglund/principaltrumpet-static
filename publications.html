Total Trumpet

Total trumpet written by Michael Davis features 13 energizing etudes covering all aspects of contemporary trumpet playing. These comprehensive studies run the gamut of styles and approaches: scales, intervals, four part chorale, jazz phrasing, rock horn section, upper and lower registers, lead playing, etc. The accompanying CD, which includes demonstrations by Phil Smith, Randy Brecker and Jim Hynes, enables you to play along with a trumpet quartet, an acoustic rhythm section, a rock horn section, a big band brass section, as well as contemporary synthesizer tracks. An important resource for all trumpet players.

Concert Studies

CONCERT STUDIES is written by the following composers: James Barnes, Stephen Bulla, Douglas Court, James Curnow, Timothy Johnson, James L. Hosay, Kevin Norbury, Philip Sparke, Joseph Turrin, and Allen Vizzutti. These new concert etudes for trumpet were composed especially for Phil, and are all challenging in different ways. They combine lyrical and technical playing with a full range from low F# to high D. They are best-suited for the intermediate to advanced player. Also included is a demonstration CD of Phil playing each of these studies. This publication belongs in every private trumpet lesson studio and on each serious player's personal library shelves.

Advanced Concert Studies

Advanced Concert Studies for Trumpet contains the same musical and technical challenges found in the original Concert Studies for Trumpet, only on a more advanced level. Lyricism is a large component of this book, with technical challenges of range, tonguing, intervals, and key signatures abounding. More advanced players will use this book in its suggested transpositions. Also included is a CD of Philip Smith performing the following studies:

Tangents (James Curnow) / Crazy Clock (Allen Vizzutti) / Etude Fantasy (Stephen Bulla) / Lyric Piece (Gregory Pascuzzi) / Scherzino (James Barnes) / Auburn (David Sampson) / Declaration (Douglas Court) / Waltz (Phil Snedecor) / Attitudes (Timothy Johnson) / Folk Dance (Eric Ewazen) / Street Song (Phil Snedecor) / Romance Etude (Joseph Turrin) / Zing! (Allen Vizzutti) / Dance Etude (Joseph Turrin) / Spinoff No. 1 (Ellen Taaffe Zwilich) / Impulse! (James Curnow)

Three bonus studies are included:

Coronation Fanfare (Joseph Turrin) / Spinoff No. 2 (Ellen Taaffe Zwilich) / Chambers (Bruce Broughton)
