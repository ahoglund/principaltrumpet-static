The Bible Says...

Can I just leave God and Jesus alone?

The Bible tells us that we have a choice to either accept or reject God and His Son. In Joshua 24:14-15 we read, "Now fear the LORD and serve Him with all faithfulness. Throw away the gods your forefathers worshiped beyond the River and in Egypt and serve the LORD. But if serving the LORD seems undesirable to you, then choose for yourselves this day whom you will serve, whether the gods your forefathers served beyond the River, or the gods of the Amorites, in whose land you are living. But as for me and my household, We will serve the LORD." In I Kings 18:21 we read, "Elijah went before the people and said, "How long will you waiver between two opinions? If the LORD is God, follow Him; but if Baal is God, follow Him." See also Philippians 2:9-11, Dueteronomy 30:19-20, Psalm 14:1
